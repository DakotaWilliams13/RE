Resident Evil

Enjoy the media franchise by Capcom based on a series of exciting video games with a gripping storyline.

If you've ever heard of the survival horror genre, you should know that it owes its existence to the Resident Evil series of games. Back in 1996, Capcom introduced PlayStation players to the twisted corridors of a mansion with cannibal zombies, zombie dogs that crush everything in their path, and a collection of deadly bio-organic weapons, or B.O.W.s.

Resident Evil combined elements of action, exploration, and resource management, while still keeping players terrified, and a whole new genre of gaming was born.

The game immediately became a sensation and spawned numerous sequels and spin-offs, and even successfully crossed over into the film industry, comics, and animation.
